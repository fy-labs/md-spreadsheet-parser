# Contributing to md-spreadsheet-parser

Thank you for your interest in contributing to `md-spreadsheet-parser`! We welcome contributions from everyone.

## Getting Started

To start developing, please refer to our [Developer Guide](DEVELOPMENT.md) for instructions on setting up your environment, running tests, and building the project.

## How to Contribute

1.  **Fork the repository** on GitHub.
2.  **Clone your fork** locally.
3.  **Create a new branch** for your feature or bug fix.
4.  **Make your changes** and ensure tests pass (see [DEVELOPMENT.md](DEVELOPMENT.md)).
5.  **Commit your changes** with clear, descriptive messages.
6.  **Push to your fork** and submit a **Pull Request**.

## Reporting Issues

If you find a bug or have a feature request, please open an issue on the GitHub repository. Provide as much detail as possible to help us understand and resolve the issue.
